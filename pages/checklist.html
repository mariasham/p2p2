Travel Checklist 🧳

The small things are usually what people forget. Having everything ready makes your trip smoother, calmer, and way less stressful.

✈️ A good trip usually starts before you even leave. Being prepared gives you more time to actually enjoy where you are.

📋 Before You Leave

  • Passport or ID
  • Flight confirmation
  • Hotel reservation
  • Travel insurance
  • Emergency contacts
  • Download offline maps

👕 Packing Essentials

  • Clothes for the weather
  • Toiletries
  • Chargers
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Medications
  • Sunglasses / accessories

🌸 Extras

  • Snacks
  • Headphones
  • Portable charger
  • Backup cash/card
  • Camera
  • Journal or notebook

Why Checklists Matter

Traveling already comes with enough unpredictability. The more prepared you are beforehand, the more freedom you have once you arrive.
