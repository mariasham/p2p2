Your Itinerary ✈️

This is where everything stops feeling scattered and starts making sense — day by day, at your pace.

✦ The best itineraries don’t try to fill every second. They leave room for wandering, rest, and the unexpected moments that end up becoming your favorite memories.

Sample 5-Day Trip

Day 1 ✈️

Arrival

Land, settle in, explore nearby cafés, and let yourself adjust slowly.

Day 2 🌎

Explore

Walk around, discover local spots, and start understanding the rhythm of the city.

Day 3 🎟️

Experiences

Plan something memorable — a museum, cooking class, beach day, or guided tour.

Day 4 ☁️

Slow Day

Sleep in, wander aimlessly, and leave space for the unexpected.

Day 5 🌅

Wrap Up

Revisit your favorite place, take photos, and end the trip intentionally.

Planning Tips ✦

🕊️ Don’t Overbook

Leave room for spontaneous moments instead of filling every hour.

📍 Group by Area

Explore nearby places together instead of wasting time traveling back and forth.

🌤️ Stay Flexible

Weather changes, energy changes, and sometimes the best plans are the unplanned ones.

Example Daily Flow

☀️ Morning

Coffee, breakfast, and one planned activity.

🌤️ Afternoon

Explore neighborhoods, museums, shopping, or local food spots.

🌙 Evening

Slow dinners, sunsets, and quieter moments.

Your trip should feel like you.

Not rushed. Not forced. Just thoughtfully planned enough to let you actually enjoy it.
