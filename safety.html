Stay Aware ✦ Travel Better

Travel Safety

Feeling safe changes everything. When you know what to look for, you move differently — calmer, more aware, and actually able to enjoy where you are.

Before You Go ✈️

Planning

Research Your Destination

Learn local customs, transportation systems, neighborhoods, and common scams before arriving.

Documents

Save Important Information

Keep digital and physical copies of your passport, bookings, insurance, and emergency contacts.

Arrival

Plan Transportation Ahead

Know how you're getting from the airport, train station, or port before you land.

While You're There 🌍

Awareness

Stay Present

Pay attention to your surroundings instead of walking distracted with your phone out.

Essentials

Keep Valuables Secure

Use crossbody bags, zippered pockets, and avoid carrying everything at once.

Intuition

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels uncomfortable, leave the situation instead of doubting yourself.

Extra Precautions 🔒

Communication

Share Your Location

Let someone you trust know your itinerary, especially when traveling alone.

Transportation

Use Trusted Services

Stick to verified rideshare apps, official taxis, or hotel transportation.

Emergency

Know Emergency Numbers

Save local emergency services, embassy locations, and important contacts.

Quick Reminders ✦

Keep Backup Cash

Carry a small emergency amount separately.

Charge Your Phone

Portable chargers matter more than people expect.

Blend In

Confidence and awareness make a difference.
