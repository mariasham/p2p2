Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my booking later?

Usually yes. Most bookings can be adjusted before your trip, depending on the provider and timing.

Are payments secure?

This is a demo website, but real travel platforms use encrypted and protected payment systems.

Where should I start planning?

Start broad first — choose a destination, build an itinerary, then move into flights and stays.

How far in advance should I book?

Flights and hotels are usually cheaper when booked earlier, especially during busy seasons.

Can I save trips for later?

Yes. Your saved trips help you compare destinations, revisit ideas, and plan when the timing feels right.

What if I don’t know where to go?

That’s more common than you think. Exploring destinations slowly is part of the process.
