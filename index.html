Go somewhere that changes you, not just your feed.

It is so hard to leave, until you leave. And then it is the easiest thing in the world. Being on the edge of the continent, at the edge of the world, where the sea meets the sky, you realize how small you are, and how big the world is. Travel isn’t always pretty. It isn’t always comfortable. Sometimes it hurts, it even breaks your heart. But that’s okay. The journey changes you; it should change you. It leaves marks on your memory, on your consciousness, on your heart, and on your body. You take something with you. And hopefully, you leave something good behind, too.
